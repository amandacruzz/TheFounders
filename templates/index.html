About App

Online Lost and Found

Gator Lost is for gators at the University of Florida who misplace personal items on campus. Unlike your typical lost and found our website will allow students to look through lot items from the comfort of their own home. Items that have not been claimed for over a month will get donated to the St. Francis House, a homeless shelter here in gainesville.
