About Us

We're a group of University of Florida students wanting o solve an age old problem for every student on campus: Losing your stuff. Whether it be your computer charger, or your favorite gameday bandana (Go Gators!), we aim to make it easier to connect a lost item with its owner. Do not hesitate to reach out to any of us if you have any questions or suggestions!
