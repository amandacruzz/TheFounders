About Us

I joined the Hex community 1 year ago. HexTrack is my contribution back to this great community. I seen a need to track my own portfolio but current apps are slow to implement new coins; hence why HexTrack was born. I invested a lot of time and a bit of money creating something I believed people of this community could use/benefit from. If you enjoy using this webapp, please show your appreciation by following us on Twitter @Hextrackapp. If you have any suggestions or would like us to add a certain coin for you, please visit our "Contact Us" page. If you'd like to contribute to the growth of HexTrack, please consider sharing our social media handles or donate to support the cost of running such application.'
